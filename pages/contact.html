FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about hygiene rules, checkout steps, or merchant analytics? Find quick detailed answers below.

What is SaveBite and how does it save food?
SaveBite is a dynamic static web portal that connects local food business (cafes, restaurants, bakeries) with consumers. Restaurants upload daily high-quality edible surpluses (like unsold bread, closing hour lunch boxes) at discounted rates up to 70% off. Users purchase bundles on our platform and collect them directly during designated pickup times, preventing food waste.
Is the surplus food completely safe and hygienic?
Absolutely! All food listed on SaveBite consists of fresh surplus items that were prepared today but simply did not sell because of standard commercial buffer stocks or cafe shelf-rotation quotas. We maintain strict hygiene agreements with our partners, mandating that food is kept in perfect storage conditions prior to consumer collection.
How do I collect the food I purchased?
Once you confirm a surplus purchase, you will receive a digital checkout confirmation on screen showing the restaurant's address and designated collection window. Simply walk into the store during that time, present your digital recipe screen to the counter staff, collect your pack, and enjoy your meal!
How can restaurants or cafes register as partners?
Businesses can simulate surplus listing registration right here on our "For Business" dashboard simulator tab! To officially join, submit your merchant interest details via the contact form above, selecting "Merchant Partner Registration", and our ecological onboarding team will reach out immediately.
